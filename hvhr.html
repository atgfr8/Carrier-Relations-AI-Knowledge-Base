High Value / High Risk Cargo (HVHR)

This page provides internal guidance for identifying and handling shipments that carry elevated theft, compliance, or operational risk.

Common HVHR Cargo

  • Metals (copper, aluminum, scrap)
  • ATVs / UTVs / recreational vehicles
  • Electronics (TVs, computers, components)
  • Pharmaceuticals / medical goods
  • Retail goods with high resale value
  • Any easily transferable or offloaded freight

Risk Factors to Evaluate

  • Declared cargo value
  • Theft attractiveness
  • Ease of unloading or resale
  • Load visibility (blind shipments vs labeled)
  • Carrier tracking capability (ELD, GPS)

Operational Guidance

  • Require active ELD or tracking when applicable
  • Verify carrier history and equipment
  • Escalate uncertain or borderline shipments
  • Avoid assumptions on exemption eligibility
⚠️ If cargo classification is unclear or appears high-risk, escalate for review instead of proceeding with standard approval.
