High Value High Risk Cargo (HVHR)

This page is for internal reference on cargo types that may require added review, added tracking expectations, or may not qualify for standard exemptions.

Common HVHR Examples

  • Metals
  • ATVs / UTVs / off-road vehicles
  • Electronics
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Easily offloaded high-theft cargo

What to Review

  • Cargo type
  • Declared cargo value
  • Theft risk
  • Whether cargo is easily resold or easily removed
  • Whether policy requires an active ELD connection

Internal Guidance

When in doubt, treat uncertain cargo as higher risk and escalate for review rather than assuming it qualifies.
