High Value / High Risk Cargo (HVHR)

This page provides internal guidance for identifying and handling shipments that carry elevated theft, compliance, or operational risk.

Common HVHR Cargo

  • Metals (copper, aluminum, scrap)
  • ATVs / UTVs
  • Electronics
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Retail / resale goods

Risk Factors

  • High declared value
  • Ease of theft or resale
  • Unsecured or untracked freight
  • No ELD / tracking visibility

Guidance

  • Require tracking when possible
  • Verify carrier history
  • Escalate unclear cases
⚠️ If unsure, escalate — do not assume eligibility.
