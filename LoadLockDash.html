Alert triage filter

After reading the alert description, answer these questions to determine risk level and routing. Equipment type is your strongest signal — dry van and reefer account for nearly all cargo theft events.

All alerts Universal response steps
▼
1
What is the equipment type?
Equipment type is one of the strongest early indicators of theft risk.
Flatbed: is the cargo metals, ATVs / UTVs / off-road vehicles, or anything easily offloaded?
2
Is the commodity high-value or theft-prone?
Electronics, pharmaceuticals, alcohol, tobacco, food grade, metals. Unknown commodity — treat as yes.
3
Are there any identity or fraud signals on the carrier?
Active identity alert, spoofed email or domain, fraudulent contact, flagged VOIP number.
4
Is this lane outside the carrier's known operating area?
No observed operations in the origin state in the past year.
