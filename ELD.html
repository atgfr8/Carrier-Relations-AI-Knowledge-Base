This tool is intended as a guide to help evaluate ELD exemption eligibility based on current policy. Results may not account for every situation, and may contain errors. All results must be reviewed and confirmed by a qualified team member. No override should be granted or denied without human review.
The goal is to limit ELD exemption overrides to those that are legitimate under this policy. Granting an ELD exemption — even for a legitimate reason — negates ping-at-pickup protection for the full duration of the override.
